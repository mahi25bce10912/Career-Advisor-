Problem Statement:
1. Students struggle to connect academic performance with career choices
2. No simple tool to visualize subject strengths and get career guidance
3. Manual career counseling is time-consuming and expensive
4. Need for instant, data-driven career suggestions based on academic strengths

Scope of the Project:
1. Analyze student marks across 5 core subjects
2. Identify strongest academic area automatically
3. Provide relevant career recommendations
4. Visualize performance through charts and graphs
5. Simple, single-user desktop application
6. No database storage or user accounts

Target Users:
1.High school students exploring career options
2.College students considering specialization
3.Career counselors for quick initial assessment
4.Parents helping children with career planning
5.Teachers identifying student strengths

High-level Features:
1.Subject-wise marks input with validation
2.Automatic strongest subject identification
3.Career mapping based on subject excellence
4.Performance classification (Excellent/Good/Average/Needs Improvement)
5.Interactive data visualization (bar and pie charts)
6.Instant career recommendations with salary estimates
